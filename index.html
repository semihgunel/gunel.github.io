Semih Günel

I graduated with my Ph.D. at EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland in 2022. I did my Ph.D. in Computer Vision Lab under the supervision of Pascal Fua. Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey. I'm interested in 3D computer vision, self-supervised learning and modeling time series.

Soon, I will start at Meta London as a Machine Learning Engineer.
