Semih Günel

I am a fourth year doctoral candidate at EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland. I am doing a joint Ph.D. at Computer Vision Lab and Neuroengineering Lab. I'm interested in computer vision, machine learning, modeling time series, and solving biological problems using data-driven methods.

Before that, I obtained my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey.

My offices are at BC 301 and AI 1125.
